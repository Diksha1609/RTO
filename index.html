Clotter - Ensuring Tractor Safety Through Red Cloth Detection

Red Cloth Detection Behind Tractors

Clotter makes it easy to ensure tractor safety by detecting the presence of red cloth behind the tractor. Simply set up Clotter to start receiving real-time alerts.

Camera Tracker

Clotter uses advanced camera systems to efficiently detect the presence of red cloth behind tractors. Our high-performance cameras capture real-time images, and with the help of deep learning algorithms, the system analyzes these images to identify whether the red cloth is visible.

Penalty for Missing Red Cloth

If the red cloth is not detected on your tractor, you will be fined for not adhering to safety protocols. The red cloth is essential for ensuring visibility and preventing accidents during operations.
